- PPO insurance plans will often accept superbills from out-of-network private practice therapists and allow you to receive partial compensation for session fees. You would be responsible for submitting these superbills to your insurance company.
- It may be helpful for you to reach out to your insurance company first to determine how much they can reimburse you.

- Therapy is a financial, time, and emotional investment in you.
- Individual Sessions (45 to 50 minutes): $200
- Couples Sessions (50 to 60 minutes): $215
- Family Sessions (50 to 60 minutes): $215
- I offer a limited number of sliding scale spots based on need, and I provide reduced fees for multiple times a week therapy.
- How do you accept payment? Session can be paid by debit, credit, FSA, or HSA cards. I also accept payments via Zelle.
- Session can be paid by debit, credit, FSA, or HSA cards. I also accept payments via Zelle.
- What is your cancellation policy? I ask for at least 48 hours of cancellation notice prior to our scheduled session by means of email or voicemail. If I do not receive adequate notice, the full session fee will be charged. However, there will be no late cancellation charge if we are able to reschedule within the same session period.

- What is the first session like? Prior to the initial session, I will send you a link to set up your client portal. Here you can access and fill out paperwork for us to begin our work together. At your session, we will review questions you had about the paperwork, spend time getting to know you, address what brings you into therapy, and set up goals for treatment. The first several sessions allow me to get a sense of what has been going on for you and what we will be addressing in treatment.
