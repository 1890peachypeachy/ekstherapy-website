### Key body copy

- Life can be filled with many stressors impacting our mental, emotional, physical, and relational health. No matter where you are in life, you are not meant to go through it alone.
- Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist
- Hello. I am glad you are here in your first step in your journey towards healing and growth. It is my privilege to join alongside you in paving your life towards health, healing, and wholeness.
- Starting therapy can be daunting. You’ve already taken a brave first step looking into therapy. I encourage you to take another step to reach out. Therapy could be a safe and non-judgmental space you need - one where you can develop a deep understanding of yourself and find relief from the emotional pain you carry.
- The logo is comprised of two flowers - a lotus and a marigold. The Lotus flower is seen as the perfect analogy for the human condition where even when its roots are in the dirtiest of waters, it produces the most beautiful flower. It symbolizes compassion, resilience, transformation, and rebirth. The marigold flower represents power, strength, and the light that lives inside a person.
- My hope is that you would come to experience and embody these various milestones within yourself as we journey towards health, healing, and wholeness.
