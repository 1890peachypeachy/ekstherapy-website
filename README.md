## About

Esther K. Shin Therapy offers compassionate, trauma-informed, and culturally sensitive relational therapy for individuals (teens & adults), couples, and families. Specializing in anxiety, depression, identity development, trauma, grief, Asian American issues, couples counseling, and more.
