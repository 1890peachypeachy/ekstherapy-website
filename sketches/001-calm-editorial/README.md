## Variant: Calm Editorial

### Design stance
Warm, parchment-toned, editorial therapist site with a strong hero message and large human portrait.

### Key choices
- **Layout:** Split hero: copy/CTA on the left, large portrait on the right.
- **Typography:** Georgia serif display headings with system sans UI/body.
- **Color:** Parchment, ivory, warm brown, muted terracotta.
- **Interaction:** Button press feedback, card hover lift, sketch-only toast.

### Trade-offs
- **Strong at:** Trust, warmth, calm professionalism, clear homepage journey.
- **Weak at:** Less dramatic than photo-first; more “premium editorial” than original Squarespace.

### Best for
A polished final site that feels therapist-appropriate, modern, warm, and conversion-aware without feeling salesy.
