## Variant: Conversion-Focused Cards

### Design stance
Practical, decision-oriented therapy website: make fit, fees, location, and next step obvious immediately.

### Key choices
- **Layout:** Above-the-fold card system with decision facts, photo, fees, services, and contact flow.
- **Typography:** Serif headings with clean sans-serif operational details.
- **Color:** Warm neutrals with dark practical info strip.
- **Interaction:** Form toast, pressable CTAs, card-like scanning.

### Trade-offs
- **Strong at:** UX clarity, conversion, practical information, reducing uncertainty.
- **Weak at:** Less poetic/emotional; could feel more “clinic website” if not softened with imagery.

### Best for
A final site optimized for clients who need quick answers before reaching out: price, fit, office, teletherapy, and what happens next.
