## Variant: Photo-Forward Magazine

### Design stance
Closer to the original Squarespace feel: emotional, photography-led, warm, spacious, and more dramatic.

### Key choices
- **Layout:** Full-bleed hero image with overlaid message and CTA.
- **Typography:** Large serif headline over photography; magazine-like section rhythm.
- **Color:** Warm photo overlays, cream surfaces, deep brown section.
- **Interaction:** Image zoom on service cards, sketch-only mini form toast.

### Trade-offs
- **Strong at:** First impression, visual warmth, preserving original site’s photographic identity.
- **Weak at:** More dependent on photo quality/crop; can be less immediately scannable than the card version.

### Best for
A site that should feel emotionally rich, boutique, and closer to the original Squarespace design language.
